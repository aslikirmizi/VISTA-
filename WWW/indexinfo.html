What is VISTA+?

VISTA+ is an abbreviation for "Visualization of Scanpath Trend Analysis Plus". The core purpose of VISTA+ is to provide researchers who are working in eye tracking area or related subfields the visualization of their eye tracking data sets, then perform the Scanpath Trend Analysis (STA) algorithm on resulting visualization style by choosing their Areas of Interests (AOIs) to discover the most trending path on the current stimulus. Furthermore, the user can choose to animate the STA result for a better understanding of the most trending path.
